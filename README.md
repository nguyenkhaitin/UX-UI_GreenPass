> *A digital service solution promoting sustainable travel and eco-conscious experiences through advanced UX/UI design and system analysis.*

Developed for the **Information Services** course at the **University of Social Sciences and Humanities (USSH)**, GreenPass is a mobile application designed to connect eco-travelers with sustainable destinations. This project demonstrates a deep integration of Business Analysis and Product Design, transforming complex service requirements into a seamless digital experience.

---

### My Role: UX/UI Designer & Business Analyst
I led the end-to-end design and analysis process, bridging the gap between user experience and technical system architecture:

* **UX Research & Strategy:** Conducted deep-dive user research to build **User Personas** and **Customer Journey Maps**, identifying critical pain points in the sustainable tourism market.
* **Business Process Modeling:** Designed the **Service Blueprint** and **BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation)** to map out the complex interactions between front-stage user actions and back-stage system operations.
* **Requirements Analysis:** Developed a **Business Functional Decomposition (BFD)** to break down high-level system requirements into modular, implementable features for the development team.
* **High-Fidelity Prototyping:** Directly designed **28+ interactive mobile screens** in **Figma**, covering all business scenarios—from eco-destination discovery to green certification verification—ensuring a seamless and professional UI/UX.
---

### Key Objectives
* **Eco-System Integration:** Connect travelers, local businesses, and environmental organizations through a single digital platform.
* **Service Transparency:** Use Service Blueprints to ensure clear operational visibility across all service touchpoints.
* **User-Centric Design:** Deliver a high-fidelity prototype that validates the feasibility of a sustainable travel mobile app.
